Welcome to Algarvio Charity, where we empower families and honour lives by providing compassionate support in life's most challenging moments. Join us in making a difference.

We are dedicated to providing compassionate, dignified support to individuals and families facing end-of-life care, regardless of age, religion, or circumstance. Through pro-bono legal assistance, mental health support, and culturally inclusive funeral services, we strive to ensure that no one faces this journey alone.
