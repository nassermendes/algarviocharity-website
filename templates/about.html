About Algarvio Charity

Our Mission

At Algarvio Charity, we are committed to providing compassionate and inclusive end-of-life care for individuals and families, regardless of age, religion, or circumstance. Our mission is captured in our tagline: "Empowering Families, Honouring Lives." We believe that dignity and care should never be compromised by financial barriers, which is why we offer our services without imposing fees or quotas.

Our Values

Compassion

We provide support with empathy and understanding during life's most challenging moments, ensuring families can focus on what truly matters—honouring their loved ones.

Dignity

We ensure respectful and dignified treatment for all individuals and families we serve, prioritizing their stories, needs, and cultural practices.

Inclusivity

We welcome and support all people, creating an environment where every life is celebrated and every family feels supported, regardless of their background or beliefs.

Support

We provide comprehensive support through our network of pro bono professionals—solicitors, therapists, and funeral directors—who are passionate about making a difference.

How We Operate

Our model is built on a spirit of collaboration. Families who benefit from our services are encouraged to contribute their skills or resources back to the charity, creating a sustainable cycle of care and support. We work with a growing network of pro bono professionals who share our commitment to making a difference in people's lives.
